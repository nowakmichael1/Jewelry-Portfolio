About Me

My name is Michael Brian Nowak. I am a second-generation jeweler from New York City, born in 1993. Since childhood, I have been drawn to art, history, and craftsmanship. My inspiration comes from history, animation, nature, the city, music, and other artists. In addition to my passion for jewelry, I am a musician, skateboarder, and fantasy enthusiast. My work reflects a deep appreciation for detail and storytelling, blending timeless techniques with modern design.
